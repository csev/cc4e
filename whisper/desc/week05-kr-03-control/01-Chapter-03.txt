C Control Flow Statements: Chapter 3 of 1978 Kernighan and Ritchie Book

control flow, C, if-else, switch, while, for, do-while, break, continue, go to, labels

This lecture covers Chapter 3 of the 1978 C Programming book by Kernighan and Ritchie. It explains control flow statements including statements and blocks, if/else constructs, else if patterns, switch statements, and loop structures (while, for, do while).

The lecture also details break and continue statements, as well as go to and labels, highlighting their historical use in C and the book's significance in the evolution of portable C applications during the 1970s.
